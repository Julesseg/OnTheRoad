First release of OnTheRoad.

- Plan road trips day by day with places, stays, meals, activities, and notes.
- See your whole route on a map with road-following directions.
- Search for places or share them in from Maps and Safari.
- Open any address in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze.
- Cover photos, per-item checklists, light/dark, English and French.
- Local-first: everything stays on your device. No account, works offline.
